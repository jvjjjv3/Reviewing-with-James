Have a Frightful Halloween |10/31/23

From the eerie tales of witches and werewolves to the haunting stories of ghosts, this time of year is filled with spine-tingling surprises, and the occasional 'Boo!' just adds to the thrill. You aren't shaking, right? I hope I didn't scare you. I just love this time of year!

What is Halloween, you may ask? Celebrated every year on October 31st, it is a day unlike any other. It involves dressing up in costumes, trick-or-treating (going door to door and asking for candy), and decorating with spooky themes. Halloween is especially popular among children and adults alike.

Every year, people choose a costume to wear during the day. This was a tradition that dates back all the way to ancient Celtic and European times. It was a time of Social Celebration. Some popular options are: Classic Monsters, Horror, Animals, and Pop Culture Icons. Lots of people have Halloween parties where people can go and show off their costumes! It's a fun time to socialize and make new friends. There's also some cool activities that people like to play at these parties, such as bobbing for apples, carving pumpkins, or playing spooky games!. My favorite thing to do on Halloween is eat all the candy! What's yours?.

I hope everybody has a safe and enjoyable Halloween!!! Make sure to get lots of candy!
