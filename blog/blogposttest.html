Take Me Out to the Ball Game | 10/2/23

Hello everyone and welcome to our first official blog post! It truly is an honor to be here today. I thought long and hard about what our topic should be, and I couldn’t be more happy about it. Let’s talk about America’s favorite pastime, baseball. Who doesn’t love baseball, am I right?

Let’s dive in to when this beautiful game was invented. There was no single person to invent the sport, however, it actually evolved from older bat-and-ball games. These games date back to several hundreds, if not thousands of years ago! Very similar to how baseball works today, the objective of these bat-and-ball games was to hit a ball with a bat and then run around a set of bases or markers. These games were more for recreational purposes though. Over time, baseball became more professionalized, just like how it is today!

Throughout baseball’s history, there have been many great players to play the game on the big stage. Big named players such as Joe DiMaggio, Babe Ruth, Jackie Robinson, Derek Jeter, Aaron Judge, Lou Gehrig, and Mickey Mantle. These players were very talented and adored by many fans across the globe. Some of the most iconic baseball teams include The New York Yankees, Chicago White Sox, and the Detroit Tigers.
